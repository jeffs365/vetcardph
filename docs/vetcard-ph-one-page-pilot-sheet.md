# VetCard 2-Week Comeback Reminder Pilot

Recover missed vaccine, deworming, grooming, and follow-up visits from existing clinic records.

---

## What VetCard Does

VetCard helps clinics find pets that are due or overdue, prepare reminder messages, and track whether owners reply, book, or come back.

This is not a full system migration. It starts with a small pilot batch.

---

## How The Pilot Works

1. Clinic sends 10 to 30 selected records.
2. VetCard encodes the basic details.
3. VetCard finds due or overdue pets.
4. Clinic approves the reminder messages.
5. Reminders are sent through the agreed channel.
6. VetCard tracks replies, bookings, and return visits.
7. Clinic receives a simple 2-week result report.

---

## Records Needed

Only basic reminder details:

- pet name
- owner first name or nickname
- mobile number or Messenger contact
- service type
- last vaccine, deworming, grooming, or follow-up date
- next due date if available

No full medical history is needed for the first pilot.

---

## Pilot Output

- due and overdue list
- reminder messages
- reply tracker
- booking/visit tracker
- estimated recovered revenue
- simple result report
